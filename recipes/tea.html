Tea

Tea is an extremely popular drink among people in south asian country.
Although it was brought by british people to India during colonisation,
the way how it is prepared has been experimented and changed over decades.
Here is the complete recipe to make the perfect cup of tea.

Ingrediants

  • milk (3/4) cup
  • water (1/4) cup
  • sugar (1tbsp)
  • processed tea (1tbsp)

Instruction

Step 1 : pour water in a vessel

Step 2 : add tea and let it boil until you see bubbles in the vessel

Step 3 : add sugar and let it dissolve

step 4 : add milk at last and let it boil until the texture of the tea become skin like color. serve it hot
