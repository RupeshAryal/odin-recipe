Odin Recipe

Running out of idea on what to have for you daytime snacks??
we got you covered. Chatpat might be the snack you have been missing.

ingrediants

  • 2 boiled potato
  • 1 finely cut onion
  • puffed rice
  • waiwai noodle
  • lemon
  • oil
  • turmeric, coriander-cumin masala

instruction

step 1: cut boiled potatoes and add it in a bowl along with chopped onions

step 2: add coriander-cumin powder, salt and turmeric and mix all of them together

step 3: add waiwai instant noodle and the spices that came in the packet, mix everthing together and add 1 tbsp of oil

step 4: once you are done mixing everything now its time to add puffed rice

step 5: finally add lemon and mix everything again. Its time to server the snacks.
