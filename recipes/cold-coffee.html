Odin Recipe

cold coffee can be the best way to recharge yourself in hot summer days
its easy to make and requires less ingrediants to prepare.

Ingrediants

  • ice
  • coffee
  • milk
  • ice cream (optional)

instruction

step 1 : boil milk and let it cool

step 2 : add half spoon coffee in a cup and add a tbsp of milk, mix it well until you make a froth out of it

step 3 : add milk to the froth, mix it gently

step 4 : add ice in the glass, add a scoop of you favourite ice-cream and enjoy the drink.
